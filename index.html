About

Strategy first.
Execution always.

I'm a Minneapolis-based marketing professional with experience spanning brand partnerships, integrated campaigns, digital strategy, and B2B marketing — built across some of the most dynamic companies in entertainment, gaming, and sports.

What ties it all together is a consistent approach: start with the audience, map the journey, and measure what moves the needle. Whether I'm negotiating a $17M media portfolio with the NBA, leading a $1M website redesign, or designing a sweepstakes campaign before playoff matchups are set, I bring the same rigor to every project.

I'm currently exploring Integrated Marketing Manager and client/account marketing roles where I can bring this breadth of experience to a brand or agency team — particularly those working in retail, consumer products, or entertainment. I'm also actively investing in where the industry is heading: I recently completed a Digital Marketing Certificate from Gonzaga University with a focus on AI-powered campaign execution and generative AI for marketing.
